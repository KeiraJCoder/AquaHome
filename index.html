About AquaHouse

AquaHouse is a unique underwater wonderland where you can explore a house that has been transformed into an aquarium. Our collection of fish comes from all over the world, and we've even bred our own new species of fish - the AquaHouse guppy! Come visit us and see our fishy friends up close.



Tours

We offer guided tours of AquaHouse for all ages. Tours are available every day, and last for approximately one hour. Our guides will take you through the house, pointing out all the different species of fish and answering any questions you may have. We even provide scuba gear and underwater cameras for those who want to get up close and personal with our fishy friends.
